# Privacy Policy for DevJSON

**Last Updated: March 26, 2026**

Your privacy is paramount. DevJSON is designed to be a local-only tool and operates under the primary principle of **Data Locality**.

### 1. No Data Collection
DevJSON does not collect, store, or transmit any user information. All data processing—including parsing JSON/XML/YAML, searching, and computing structural diffs—occurs entirely within your local browser environment.

### 2. Permissions & Usage
- **Website Content**: The extension requires permission to read the text content of the active tab. This is used solely to detect if the page contains raw data structures (JSON, XML, or YAML) so that the interactive explorer can be rendered.
- **Local Interaction**: Any modifications you make in the "Live Editor" (V2) or data you paste for comparison remain within your current session and are never sent to any external servers.

### 3. Data Transfer
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: Since we do not collect any data, we do not sell or transfer any user information to third parties.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use tracking pixels, cookies, or analytics services that monitor your usage of the extension.

### 4. Security
DevJSON operates within the security sandbox of the Chrome browser. It does not use remote code or external scripts, ensuring the integrity of your browsing experience.

### 5. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository.
