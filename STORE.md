# Chrome Web Store Description: DevJSON

## Supercharge Your API Debugging with DevJSON

Stop squinting at raw, unformatted data. DevJSON is the premium, all-in-one explorer for developers who live in the browser. Whether you're working with JSON, XML, or YAML, DevJSON turns chaotic text into a clean, interactive, and searchable tree.

### Why DevJSON?

DevJSON isn't just another formatter. It's a structural intelligence tool designed to save you time and cognitive load.

### Key Capabilities:

- **Instantly Detect & Format**: Automatically identifies JSON, XML, and YAML. No more manual pasting into formatting websites.
- **Powerful Semantic Search**: Find what you need in seconds with real-time highlighting that works across keys and values.
- **Structural Comparison (Diff)**: Ever wonder what changed between two API responses? Upload a local file and compare it directly against the current page.
- **Aesthetic Precision**: A beautiful, distraction-free interface with premium typography and a focus on readability.
- **Developer Shortcuts**: 
    - Copy paths or values with a single click.
    - View data size and format at a glance.
    - Toggle back to raw view whenever you need the original source.

### Privacy First:
DevJSON works entirely on your machine. We never collect, store, or transmit your data. Your API responses stay between you and your server.

### Built for Performance:
Handles medium to large datasets with ease, ensuring your browser stays responsive while you explore complex nested objects.
